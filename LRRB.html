Description

Little Red Riding Block is a 2D Jump´n´Run Game in which you take control over a little block-shaped character named Little Red Riding Block.

Inspired by the well-known fairy tale, the task of the game is to maneuver the character through the forest to grandmothers house.
The player has not only to reach the end of the level in that given time, but also pick up flowers for the grandmother. To achieve that, several different types of pickups are distributed randomly through the level.

Cupcakes - Give a short dash forward
Wine - Increases time limit
Flowers - Minimum 10 Pieces - each extra flower gives extra points
Baskets - Increases max. Flower Limit
