Hello There!

My name is Jaqueline and I currently live in Cambridge, United Kingdom. Currently I am working on my own Indie Game. A cosy life sim, set in the beautiful Austrian Mountains. Before that I have worked from Junior Gameplay Programmer up to Senior Animation Programmer at Ninja Theory from 2018 till 2023.

I also have graduated as Bachelor of Science in Game Development & Augmented Reality from the University of Applied Sciences Salzburg in Austria

My experience so far heavily lies in story-rich, visually realistic single player games, making sure characters move and behave believably. I also have some decent experience in making VR Games. On a technical side, most of my experience lies within Unreal Engine. However, I have some experience in Unity and a little bit with custom engines also.
